From Studentsâ€™ Executive Meetings to Cultural Events or Morning Assemblies or CA periods for classes or talks and discussions with larger student groups the auditorium is always bustling with activity.

School Canteen

A canteen, where, during lunch period becomes a hub of social connections.

A well planned menu under the directions of our Home Science department and in consultation with our school doctor provide a rich variety of meals for students in the Nursery and Class I. The food is in the school canteen. Older children can choose from freshly cooked variety of snacks like samosa, vada, dosa, idlis, dabille and noodles. The School authorities in consultation with the PTA fix prices. The PTA sub-committee members check the quality of food and hygiene from time to time. Aerated drinks are not permitted. Instead, the children can choose from drinks like lassi, chaas, flavored milk, juices without added sugar, cold coffee, etc.

School Library

Two enormous libraries that will vastly fulfill the needs of 1600 students, 200 teachers and other staff.

Sardar Patel Vidyalaya has two libraries. The senior school library is an open shelf library surrounded by Ashoka trees, it has a modern look and is well lighted. A qualified librarian and an attendant fulfill the academic needs of 1600 students, 200 teachers and administrative staff in their project and research work. The library is well stocked with a collection of 40,000 books, subscribes to 25 of current affairs magazines and 6 daily newspapers There is a fairly large collection of Reference books. The Junior School library with a collection of 5,000 books is more informal and low tables and floor cushions provide children easy access to books.


What's New

  • Online Festivals, Activites and Classes

    In an effort to try and do its part in reducing COVID-19, the school has started taking online classes for the last one year. But even though online school lacks the physical interactions, privacy and of course- meeting our friends, the school has tried its best to make online classes as fun as physical ones. To accomplish this, the Vidyalaya has kept many of its original physical activities and celebrations (such as the Independence Day celebration, Rangoli festival, Food festival, Sardar Patel Jayanti, Activity week and countless more,) online.

  • Online Class-wise Sessions with the School Councillor

    Seeing the dramatic decrease in the students' well being and resilience, the school has started class-wise sessions with the school councillor, where she has a casual discussion with the students about their well being, resilience, feelings and more. Following the discussions, there are short activities made to try and improve the children's mental health.
